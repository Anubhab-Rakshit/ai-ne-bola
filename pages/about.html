Our
Mission

Ebola outbreaks are a significant threat to global health, and early predictions are vital for effective resource allocation, timely interventions, and containment efforts. With AI NE BOLA, we aim to provide accurate forecasts for:

  • Expected deaths in various regions.
  • Confirmed cases to inform public health planning.
  • Case Fatality Ratio (CFR) for unreported regions.

Why Choose
AI NE BOLA ?

Accuracy : Our model leverages state-of-the-art algorithms to ensure reliable predictions.
Adaptability : It is designed to handle diverse datasets and adapt to new outbreak patterns.
Impact : By predicting key outbreak metrics, we contribute to saving lives and improving containment strategies.

How
It Works

Using cutting-edge machine learning techniques , our model analyzes:

Reported death data (including gaps where data is missing).
Case Fatality Ratios (CFR) from known regions.
Geographical data (latitude and longitude) to account for regional variations.
