AI NE BOLA

Predict. Prepare. Protect.

AI NE BOLA is an innovative machine learning project designed to tackle one of the most critical challenges in public health: predicting and managing the spread of infectious diseases, specifically Ebola.

Key Features

Accurate Predictions

Harness advanced machine learning to forecast deaths, cases, and fatality rates with precision

Geographical Insights

Analyze latitude and longitude data to capture regional variations and trends.

Comprehensive Analysis

Integrate multiple data points, including deaths and CFR, for holistic forecasting.

User-Friendly Interface

Simplified outputs to help decision-makers understand and act quickly.

Impact-Driven Design

Built to empower public health strategies and save lives.

Automated Alerts

Trigger warnings for regions with high reported cases, enabling quick attention and intervention.


Revolutionizing Outbreak Management

Ebola outbreaks pose a severe threat to public health, but with AI NE BOLA, we're bringing a new level of preparedness to epidemic response. By analyzing diverse datasets and leveraging cutting-edge machine learning, our project provides actionable insights to help mitigate the impact of outbreaks.

About Us

Powered by Data,Driven by AI

At AI NE BOLA, we believe data is the key to saving lives. Our model transforms raw data—geographical coordinates, case fatality ratios, and reported deaths—into precise predictions. This empowers health organizations to act confidently, even in regions with incomplete data
